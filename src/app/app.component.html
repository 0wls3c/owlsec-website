Members of OWLsec benefit from more than just a place to learn.

What are those benefits?

  • 100% Free access to the decades of experience within OWLsec.

  • Our members have access to exclusive opportunities such as jobs and training.

Unlike the countless number of online "communities", OWLsec invests in our members. We will guide you throughout your learning journey, encouraging growth, and pushing you to challenge yourself. Our goal simple: Provide "Open Source" Knowledge To All.

Our Mission

OWLsec is determined to raise the bar for all online communities. A community should be a welcoming, positive, and supportive environment, where members are encouaged, rather than discouraged. OWLsec offers all of these essential aspects, generating individual member retention and growth.

Our Plan

Our foundation consists of members who are experienced Pentesters, Programmers, and Cyber professionals. They are the leaders, mentors, and core contributors who breath life into the OWLsec community. Acquiring more members like them is how OWLsec will grow.

Our Vision

As one of the fastest growing online communities, we understand how important it is to remain true to our values. OWLsec was made to offer one very simple service: Provide a healthy environment for like-minded individuals to learn and grow together. We will set a new standard for online learning and comaraderie.
